How can I help you today?

As a creative entrepreneur and natural health enthusiast, I am excited to help others with my knowledge, skills, and passion.

My experience in administrative and development support for nonprofits and higher education enhanced my passion for helping people. It also gave me a firm business acumen which allows me to apply my abilities to any organization’s goals and objectives. I would love to put my skills to work for your team. Please feel free to contact me to discuss.

My background in web development began in the early days of web design, when coding was limited to building tables in HTML. I proudly made a family homepage to show off my techie skills. Eventually my hobby became a side business.

Today, in the era of responsive web design (which basically means your website rocks on your phone), a firm knowledge of HTML is still very useful, although I have also learned a few new tricks. I find it satisfying to create clean code "under the hood" of any website I build and I believe that while design is important, functionality is everything! Are you looking for an updated online presence for your organization? Message me today about your web development needs! I will be happy to discuss your options.
